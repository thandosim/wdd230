Current Events

Mbabane City hosts a weekly parkrun event every saturday morning at the Golf Course. This 5km race starts at the Albert Millin Resturant and goes through the Rotary club, then the Golf course where it turns back to its starting point.

Business Spotlight

Hilton Garden Inn Mbabane

Plot 934 and 935 Mhlambanyatsi Road, Mbabane, H100, Eswatini

Hilton Garden Inn in Mbabane is located in the Central Business District near Swazi Plaza. The hotel offers an outdoor pool, restaurant, room service, and free WiFi. Nearby attractions include Sibebe Rock and Mlilwane Wildlife Sanctuary.

Hilton Garden Inn Mbabane Website

Swaziland Supply Center Mbabane

Industrial Site, LifeTech Centre, Siguca Cres, Mbabane H100, Eswatini

Swaziland Supply Centre (SSC) is a general retail and wholesale store located in Mbabane, Eswatini. They offer a wide range of quality branded products for home, office, outdoor, and industrial use. SSC has been in operation since 1969 and has multiple outlets in Mbabane and Manzini.

Swaziland Supply Center Website

Mbabane Market

Allister Miller Street, Mbabane, Hhohho H100

Mbabane Market is a vibrant and bustling market located on Allister Miller Street in Mbabane, Eswatini. It offers a variety of goods, including fresh produce, traditional crafts, and local cuisine. The market is a must-visit for tourists looking to experience local culture and find unique souvenirs
